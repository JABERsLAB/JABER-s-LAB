The TriRaptor: A Three Environmental Vehicle

A first-of-its-kind vehicle capable of operating across water, land, and air.

Project Overview

The TriRaptor is unmanned vehicle capable of operation across land, air, and sea, addressing critical limitations in existing multi-modal mobility systems. Designed for military applications, the vehicle enhances operational flexibility, adaptability, and mission success by integrating innovative propulsion, control, and structural design elements.

Future Plan

The TriRaptor is one of the most demanding and interdisciplinary projects, as it requires the integration of aerodynamics, hydrodynamics, structural design, waterproofing, buoyancy, air and underwater control, and dual-environment communication. It is still a work in progress. However, a major focus of the future development of the TriRaptor is the integration of cameras and sensors to enable vision-based control.
